Other Details

What is a Mathematical Ceremony? Phoebe and Will have organized a series of events this year that celebrate the practice of mathematics as an art and ritual. You can see the programs for previous services here. No mathematical background is expected, you won't want to miss it!

Kids: Kids are welcome at all events. We will provide a well-trusted babysitter for children under 2yo during the Liturgical Ceremony. Please indicate on your RSVP if you'd like her to watch your little one.

Dress code: Dress code for the Mathematical Ceremony is black and white; all levels of formality are welcome. Dress code for the Liturgical Ceremony and following reception is black tie, however rowing blazers (or national dress) are encouraged.

RSVP'ing: You will a receive an invitation in the mail in May. Please RSVP by June 1st.

We will continue to update this page as we approach the wedding.
